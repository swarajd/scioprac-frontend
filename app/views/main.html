This is ScioPrac

About this app

ScioPrac is an app where students from all over the country upload questions about different events, and other students can access those questions and practice with them.

Inspiration

I wanted to make this app because I thought that there wasn't enough practice that we could do. With this app students could have more practice and face a larger variety of questions.
